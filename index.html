♡ Letter for You ♡

Will you be my Valentine?

Valentine Letter: Every time I look at you, I feel lucky because you chose me, and I love you deeply. When life feels heavy, you make it light, and I love you for that. You taught me love is a choice, and I’ll choose you every day. I smile for no reason sometimes, then I remember you live in my heart. In every lifetime, I’d still find you because you’re my home. Your love healed me, and I promise to love you always. I love you most in the little moments that make you you. You’re my favorite hello, my hardest goodbye, and my greatest happiness. You make me want to be better, because you deserve the best of me. In a world of temporary things, you’re my forever.
