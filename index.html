♡ Letter for You ♡

Will you be my Valentine?

Valentine Letter: Every time I look at you, I feel endlessly lucky that you chose me. You make heavy days light, you’ve healed my heart, and you’ve taught me that love is a choice I’ll make for you every single day. In every lifetime, I’d find you, because you’re my home, my joy, my favorite hello, my hardest goodbye, my forever.
